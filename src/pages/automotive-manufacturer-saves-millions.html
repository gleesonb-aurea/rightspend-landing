The Challenge

With over 2,000 instances running across multiple regions, our client was struggling to optimize their cloud spend while maintaining the high performance needed for their critical manufacturing and design systems. Their daily cloud costs were averaging $2,200, primarily from on-demand instances.

The Immediate Impact

Within 24 hours of implementing RightSpend.ai, we observed:

  • A reduction in on-demand instance costs from $2,200 to $250 per day

  • Projected annual savings of over $255,500

How We Did It

RightSpend.ai's AI-driven platform immediately began analyzing usage patterns and identifying opportunities for optimization:

  • Automated identification of conversion candidates for reserved instances
  • Real-time workload analysis to optimize instance types and sizes
  • Intelligent scheduling of non-production workloads
  • Continuous monitoring and adjustment of resource allocation

Long-Term Benefits

Beyond the immediate cost savings, our client experienced:
