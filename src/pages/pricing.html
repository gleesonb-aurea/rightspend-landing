Pricing Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if RightSpend doesn't save me money?

If RightSpend doesn't generate verifiable cost savings, you pay nothing. Our entire business model is built on delivering measurable results. We also offer a 90-day money-back guarantee for new customers.

Are there any minimum savings requirements or monthly fees?

No minimum savings requirements or monthly fees. Whether you save $100 or $100,000 per month, you pay the same percentage. There are no fixed costs, setup fees, or ongoing charges beyond our percentage of savings.

Can I cancel RightSpend at any time?

Yes, you can cancel RightSpend at any time with 30 days' notice. There are no cancellation fees, early termination penalties, or long-term contracts. We'll help you transition back to manual management if needed.

How do you measure "net new savings"?

Net new savings are calculated by comparing your AWS costs after RightSpend implementation against your baseline spending patterns before optimization. We exclude any existing Reserved Instance or Savings Plan discounts to ensure accurate measurement.

Do RightSpend fees count toward my AWS Enterprise Discount Program (EDP)?

Yes! All RightSpend fees contribute 100% to your EDP commitment since RightSpend is built entirely on AWS infrastructure. This means your cost optimization investment also helps you achieve better EDP discount tiers.
