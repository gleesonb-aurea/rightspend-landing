Frequently Asked Questions

How does RightSpend deliver savings without lock-in?

RightSpend uses Commitment Free Discounts (CFDs), a proprietary technology that optimizes your EC2 discount coverage in real-time using Convertible Reserved Instances. Unlike traditional RIs, our AI continuously adjusts your coverage based on actual usage, eliminating waste and lock-in risk.

What does it cost?

Zero upfront costs. RightSpend charges 25% of the net new savings we generate for you. If we don't save you money, you don't pay. We also offer a 90-day money-back guarantee.

How long does implementation take?

Integration takes 2-3 days. You'll see savings start appearing on your AWS bill within 7 days of activation.

Will this conflict with my existing Savings Plans or Reserved Instances?

No! RightSpend is designed to work alongside your existing commitments. We optimize the remaining EC2 coverage that isn't already discounted, maximizing your total savings without overlap.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. No penalties, no questions asked.
