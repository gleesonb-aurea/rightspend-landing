Reserved Instance Challenges

While Reserved Instances offer significant discounts, they come with inherent challenges that many organizations struggle with:

  • ⚠️Commitment risk with changing workloads
  • ⚠️Complex capacity planning requirements
  • ⚠️Manual optimization and exchange processes
  • ⚠️Stranded commitments during migrations
  • ⚠️Limited flexibility for scaling workloads
  • ⚠️Break-even calculations and utilization tracking

AWS Savings Plans: Flexible Discount Strategy

Compute Savings Plans

Compute Savings Plans provide up to 66% discount on EC2, Lambda, and Fargate usage in exchange for a commitment to a consistent amount of compute usage (measured in $/hour) for 1 or 3 years.

✓ Advantages

  • • Automatic application across instance families
  • • Covers EC2, Lambda, and Fargate
  • • Regional flexibility
  • • Up to 66% savings vs On-Demand

EC2 Instance Savings Plans

EC2 Instance Savings Plans offer up to 72% discount but are limited to a specific instance family in a chosen region, providing less flexibility than Compute Savings Plans.

⚠️ Limitations

  • • Requires accurate capacity forecasting
  • • 1-3 year commitments required
  • • Risk of overcommitment
  • • Manual optimization required

Savings Plans Best Practices

Planning Phase

  • • Analyze 30+ days of usage data

Implementation
