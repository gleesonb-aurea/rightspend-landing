Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Commitment Free Discounts and Reserved Instances?

Commitment Free Discounts (CFDs) provide up to 55% AWS EC2 savings without requiring long-term commitments or capacity planning. Unlike Reserved Instances which require 1-3 year commitments, CFDs offer the same level of savings with complete flexibility to scale up or down anytime.

How much can I actually save with RightSpend?

RightSpend typically delivers 20-55% savings on AWS EC2 costs. The exact savings depend on your current usage patterns, existing commitments, and infrastructure setup. Most customers see immediate 30%+ reduction in their monthly AWS bills.

Is RightSpend suitable for small businesses?

Yes, RightSpend works for any organization using AWS EC2. Small businesses benefit from automated optimization without needing dedicated FinOps teams, while enterprises get advanced features like ShowBack and cost allocation.

How quickly can RightSpend be implemented?

RightSpend can be deployed in under 15 minutes with our secure, read-only AWS integration. Most customers see savings opportunities identified within 24 hours and cost reductions beginning within the first week.
