Frequently Asked Questions

Product Overview

What is RightSpend?

RightSpend is CloudFix's Commitment Free Discounts (CFDs) solution for AWS EC2 that delivers up to 55% savings vs on-demand pricing without lock-in periods. It works seamlessly with existing Savings Plans and Reserved Instances through automated cRI (Convertible Reserved Instance) optimization.

Unlike traditional AWS discount strategies that require upfront commitments, RightSpend provides enterprise-grade savings with complete flexibility to scale resources up or down as your business needs change.

How does RightSpend work?

RightSpend uses AI-driven automation to analyze your EC2 usage patterns and optimizes Convertible Reserved Instances (cRIs) in real-time. It provides FinOps-compliant discount optimization without requiring long-term commitments or changes to your infrastructure.

The platform continuously monitors your AWS environment, identifies optimization opportunities, and automatically adjusts coverage to maximize savings while maintaining operational flexibility.

How much can I save with RightSpend?

RightSpend typically delivers 20-55% savings on AWS EC2 costs compared to on-demand pricing. The exact savings depend on your current coverage mix of Savings Plans, Reserved Instances, and on-demand usage patterns.

Most customers see immediate savings within 30 days of implementation, with average savings of 35-40% for mixed workloads and up to 55% for stable, predictable usage patterns.

What makes Commitment Free Discounts different from Savings Plans?

CFDs eliminate the risk of over-committing to AWS resources. While Savings Plans require 1-3 year commitments that can become costly if your usage decreases, CFDs provide similar discount rates with zero commitment penalty.

You get the financial benefits of Reserved Instance pricing without the operational risk of capacity planning errors or changing business requirements.

Technical Integration

Can I use RightSpend alongside Savings Plans?

Yes! RightSpend is designed to complement your existing AWS discount strategy. It works seamlessly with Savings Plans, Reserved Instances, and Enterprise Discount Programs (EDP) to optimize uncovered or inefficiently covered EC2 usage.

The platform automatically identifies gaps in your current coverage and applies CFDs to maximize your overall discount rate without conflicting with existing commitments.

What are Convertible Reserved Instances (cRIs) and how does RightSpend use them?

Convertible Reserved Instances (cRIs) allow you to change instance families, operating systems, and tenancy during the term. RightSpend leverages cRIs' flexibility to provide commitment-free savings by continuously optimizing these conversions based on your actual usage patterns.

Our automated cRI management eliminates the complexity of manual conversions while ensuring you always get the best available discount rate for your current infrastructure needs.

Which AWS services and regions does RightSpend support?

RightSpend supports all major AWS EC2 instance types across all commercial AWS regions. This includes compute-optimized (C-series), memory-optimized (R-series), general purpose (M-series), and storage-optimized instances.

Cross-region optimization ensures you get the best rates regardless of where your workloads run, with automatic regional rebalancing as your usage patterns change.

How does RightSpend integrate with my existing infrastructure?

RightSpend uses secure, read-only APIs to analyze your AWS environment without requiring any changes to your existing infrastructure or applications. Integration typically takes less than 15 minutes and requires only CloudFormation template deployment.

There are no agents to install, no application modifications needed, and no disruption to your current operations.

Pricing & Contracts

No. RightSpend provides Commitment Free Discounts (CFDs) with complete flexibility. Unlike traditional Savings Plans or Reserved Instances, you can scale resources up or down without penalties or break-even concerns.
