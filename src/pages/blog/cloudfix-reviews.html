What is CloudFix AWS Cost Optimization?

CloudFix is an automated AWS cost optimization platform that continuously monitors your infrastructure, identifies savings opportunities, and applies them without manual intervention. Unlike Reserved Instances or Savings Plans that require upfront commitments and usage forecasting, CloudFix uses Commitment Free Discounts (CFDs).

Here's the key insight: Most companies overpay for AWS by 20-50% because they can't commit to 3-year Reserved Instances for dynamic workloads. CloudFix solves this by automatically applying 3-year all-upfront pricing to your on-demand EC2 instances—giving you Reserved Instance-level savings (40-55%) without the lock-in.

The platform handles the entire optimization process:

  • Continuous monitoring: Tracks your EC2 usage 24/7
  • Automatic identification: Finds instances that would benefit from CFDs
  • Seamless application: Applies savings without downtime or reconfiguration
  • Flexibility: Change or cancel anytime (no long-term contracts)
  • Verification: Reports actual savings on your AWS bill

CloudFix is owned by RightSpend, and the two platforms work together—CloudFix handles the technical optimization, RightSpend provides the customer interface and management layer.
